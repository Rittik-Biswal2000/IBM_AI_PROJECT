Social Distancing Monitoring and Alert System

An useful application to monitor social distancing


The nCoV 2019 has changed the ways we live our daily lives . Masks and Social Distancing are the new norms . Though the motto is to flatten the curve and lockdown emerges as the most viable solution yet it becomes infeasible to lockdown the country for months at times when majority of people are still dependent on their daily wages for two square meals . So , as the country opens up and business going back to their usual business we need a comprehensive solution to ensure that we minimise the spread of nCov-2019.
Keeping that in mind , I have come up with a unique solution to monitor social distancing norms being followed in public places .
This application uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) and alert systems to monitor Social Distancing .


How to Use ?

This application uses Flask on top of python to make the application as user-friendly as possible .
Just choose your video file and click on Submit Button , sit back and monitor the social distancing among people while the application automatically generates an alert system when it detects violations .

Scope :

This applicaton can easily be integrated with commercial CCTVs and survelliance cameras .
For demonstration purpose and physical constraints , this app currently uses video feed from laptop .
